ABOUT

Where does the news data come from?

The news stories are retrieved from the News API.

Where/How are the Sentiment scores achieved?

The project utilizes the python package Vader Sentiment (Valence Aware Dictionary and sEntiment Reasoner). It is particually attuned for lower word count blocks of text and becuase each story object contains a small, easily digestiable description, Vader was the perfect choice. Prior to displaying the news items on the page, each indivdual story is ran through the analyzer and three percentage scores are given depending on that story's description phrasing. All scores are then averaged and an over all sentinemt score is presented for each batch of stories at the top of the two news containers.

Why create this project?

How and where a person consumes information can play a large roll in the way that indivdual veiws the world. It's no secret that the light (positive, netural, or negative) a partuclar news segment is presented is heavily influenced by that news outlet's own biases. The goal behind the project was to utilize a tool like Vader to attempt to quantify those biases so that the reader can shape a more informed opinion.
